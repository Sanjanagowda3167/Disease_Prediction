What we predict
Diabetes Prediction
Predicts diabetes risk using 8 key medical features including glucose level, BMI, blood pressure, insulin and pregnancy history.

Best Model: Naive Bayes — 76.62% Accuracy
Heart Disease Prediction
Predicts heart disease risk using 13 clinical parameters including age, cholesterol, chest pain type, resting blood pressure, max heart rate and ST depression.

Best Model: Decision Tree — 98.54% Accuracy
How It Works
Patient data → preprocessing → feature scaling → ML model → prediction output. Models are trained on real medical datasets (Pima Indians Diabetes & UCI Heart Disease). Results show risk level and confidence score.
Evaluation Metrics
Each model is evaluated using Accuracy, Precision, Recall and F1-Score. Confusion matrices and feature-importance charts help understand model behavior and which factors most influence predictions.
